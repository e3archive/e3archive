Welcome to JEE Archive!

JEE Archive is a project which aims at preserving the history of Joint Entrance Examination (JEE). Inspired from Internet Archive, this project contains all the material for JEE preparation from the 80s to present. The intention is not to commit Copyright Infrigment, but simply to preserve the glorius history of one of the World's Toughest Examination at Highschool Level (JEE Advanced).

Intention of this website is not to commit Copyright Infrigment. This website is just to preserve the history of Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) and the Legacy of Kota & Delhi Coaching Industry.
